What is Bitlife?

Bitlife is a text-based life simulation game that allows you to live a virtual life from birth to death. Originally released as a mobile app, it has become one of the most popular life simulator games worldwide. In Bitlife, you make choices that affect your character's health, happiness, intelligence, and looks.

The game presents you with scenarios and decisions throughout your virtual life. From choosing whether to study for an exam in elementary school to deciding on a career path, getting married, having children, or even committing crimes - every choice has consequences that shape your character's destiny.
